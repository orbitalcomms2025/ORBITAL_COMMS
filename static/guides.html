Prepare in the Northern Zone

🌞 ☀️ Before the Phenomenon
📦 Prepare an emergency kit: water, food, flashlight, radio, and medicines.
🏠 Reinforce your home: clean roofs and drains.
🚧 Identify safe evacuation routes.
📲 Subscribe to early alerts.
💧 💦 During the Phenomenon
🚨 Stay calm and follow official instructions.
❌ Do not cross rivers or overflowing streams.
📡 Use a radio to receive updates if internet is down.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Help children and elderly first.
🌱 🏡 After the Phenomenon
✅ Wait for authorization before returning home.
🚱 Avoid consuming contaminated water or food.
🧹 Clean and disinfect your house.
🤝 Support your community.
